our advantages.

Electrical Repair

We guarantee the complete repair of your engine in case of failure. We know that failures are rare, but we are here if they happen.

Frame Repair

Our frames are made to last forever. But if something happens, we are happy to fix it.

Sustainable

We work with the zero waste philosophy. Any defective part is recycled and reused in another project.

Recovery

We recover stolen Bikcrafts at no additional cost to the owner. If we can't recover it, we'll give you a new one.

Security

Our insurance guarantees that all repairs are carried out using genuine, high-quality parts. Trust Bikcraft.

Speed

Your Bikcraft will be ready for use on the same day if you bring it to be repaired at one of the branches.
